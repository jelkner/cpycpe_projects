GPS Logger

By: Dylan Herreda

Introduction Materials Required boot.py datalogger.py Instructions

What you will need

  • A feather microcontroller board (M0 or m4 work best)

  • A GPS FeatherWing

  • A FeatherWing doubler

  • An active antenna (optional)

  • An Adalogger FeatherWing & an SD card (also optional). If you use this, you will need a feather tripler instead of a feather doubler.
