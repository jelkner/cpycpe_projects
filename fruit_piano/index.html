Circuit Playground Express

Fruit Piano

By: Charles B. Male and Scooter Norton

Introduction Getting started Capacitive Touch Keys Final Product

What You Will Do

You will learn how to create a Fruit Piano on the Circuit Playground Express. So when you touch a piece of fruit, it gives off lights and generates a tone.

What You Will Learn

  • How to import from Circuit Playground Express Library
  • How to use the slide switch
  • How to play sound off Circuit PLayground Express
  • How to light up neopixels
  • How to use capacitive touch pads

What you will need

  • Circuit Playground Express
  • 6 - 12 Alligator clips to male jumpers wires
  • Micro USB type B cable
  • 1-7 pieces of fruit(preferably acidic)
