Circuit Playground Express

Pendulum NeoPixels

By: Sheereen Khan

Introduction Getting Started Accelerometer Pendulum Final Product

What You Will Do

You will learn to use the accelerometer to light up the NeoPixels on the Circuit Playground Express. After getting the accelerometer on, you will be able to use the siwtch to make the pendulum effect using NeoPixels.

What You Will Learn

  • How to light up the NeoPixels according to the accelerometer.
  • Understand the movement and function of the accelerometer.
  • How to control the movement and friction to make the NeoPixels move smoothly.
